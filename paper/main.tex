\begin{abstract}
We reduce the Twin Prime Conjecture (TPC) to a single sufficient condition.
Let $\Phi_X$ denote the characteristic vector of twin primes up to $X$,
and let $R(\Phi_X)$ be the Rayleigh quotient of commutator energy over lattice energy.

\textbf{Main Theorem (Unconditional):}
\[
    \textbf{TPC} \;\iff\; R(\Phi_X) \to \infty \text{ as } X \to \infty.
\]

\textbf{Key Target $\mathbf{P(X)}$:} We identify the sufficient condition
\[
    R(\Phi_X) \geq c \cdot X^\delta \quad \text{for some } \delta > 0, \; c > 0.
\]
If $\mathbf{P(X)}$ holds, then TPC follows immediately from our Main Theorem.

\textbf{Five Attack Paths:} We present five independent approaches to proving $\mathbf{P(X)}$:
\begin{enumerate}[nosep]
    \item \emph{CV Growth:} Show $\mathrm{cv}(\xi) \to \infty$ (coefficient of variation of spectral coordinates)
    \item \emph{Eigenvalue Ratio:} Show $\lambda_{\max}/\lambda_{\min} \to \infty$
    \item \emph{Character Sum:} Show $|S(t)|/\sqrt{N} \to \infty$ where $S(t) = \sum p^{-it}$
    \item \emph{L-function:} Analyze divergence of $L_{\mathrm{twins}}(s) = \sum_{\mathrm{twin}\, p} p^{-s}$
    \item \emph{Direct Summation:} Explicit bounds on commutator energy via number-theoretic estimates
\end{enumerate}

We do not claim to prove TPC. We provide a \emph{conditional reduction} in the spirit of
Goldston--Pintz--Y{\i}ld{\i}r{\i}m: prove $\mathbf{P(X)}$ by any path, obtain TPC.
Key components---including the Main Theorem equivalence and the CV Growth path---have been
formally verified in Lean4 using the Aristotle theorem prover.
\end{abstract}
